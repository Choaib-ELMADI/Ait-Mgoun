Questions Générales

Why Ait-Mgoun is a good place to visit ?
Ait-Mgoun is known by its awesome nature over the seasons, and also different adventures during summer.
What about people situation ?
Ait-Mgoun people are a very welcoming community, as the hole moroccans are. So you will spend a good time with your family and also help us in our lives.
What was the reason for establishing the I.Y.A ? And what is it for ?
The residents of the Douar suffered from the scarcity of water and recreational activities, which led to the establishment of The Iminwarg Youth Association. Whose goal is to unite the Douar and lead it towards the best and the finest.
